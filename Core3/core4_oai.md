# Core4 OAI Axioms

This note gives a minimal physical explanation of mathematics bootstrapped from
ordered uncalibrated extents.

The aim is:

- no hidden gaps
- no arbitrary injected dimensions
- tension as lawful information
- dimensions arising when structure truly requires them

## Part I. Primitive Reality

A1. The only primitive assumption is that ordered extents exist.

A2. An extent has a recessive side and a dominant side.
That order is primitive.

A3. An uncalibrated extent is not yet a number.
It is only a distinguishable ordered span.

A4. Two extents may be related before either is calibrated.
That relation is a ratio in the broad sense: "this span against that span."

A5. Ratio precedes number.
Number is a later stabilized reading of ratio.

## Part II. Support Cells And Number

A6. A number appears when one extent is stabilized as a repeatable support
cell.

A7. The stabilized support cell is the physical meaning of the denominator.
It is not "nothing"; it is the chosen reference span counted as one.

A8. Calibration is the act of reading another extent by copies or fractions of
that support cell.

A9. Resolution is a partition of the support cell.
Ticks are declared subcells of the chosen one.

A10. If the support cell is left unpartitioned, the system still works at
unit resolution.
Ticks are not metaphysically required; they are declared grain.

A11. A calibrated value is therefore:

- an extent
- read against a stabilized support cell
- at a chosen resolution

A12. When only one side is stabilized as reference and the other remains read
upon it, the result is a rate or calibration relation, not yet a symmetric new
carrier.

## Part III. Carrier Relations

A13. If extent and support live on the same carrier, the relation is aligned.

A14. If a real distinction cannot be absorbed by the present carrier as an
aligned count, the relation is orthogonal.

A15. If the system cannot yet decide how the relation should settle, the
relation is unresolved.

A16. These three relations are the physical basis of:

- value
- structure
- generation

A17. Value is aligned calibrated participation on a settled carrier.

A18. Structure is ordered relation that is not reducible to one aligned count.

A19. Generation is lawful content produced when ordered values and ordered
structures are continued, folded, interpolated, or reconciled.

## Part IV. Birth Of Dimension

A20. Orthogonal pressure alone does not yet create a new dimension.
It only proves that the current carrier is insufficient.

A21. A new dimension appears when previously distinct supports stabilize into a
shared higher-grade basis cell.

A22. Before that stabilization, orthogonal content may appear as:

- tension
- corner
- branch
- boundary
- ray
- directed passage

but not yet as a full interior-bearing higher space.

A23. After stabilization, the new basis cell is a native positive unit family
of the higher grade.

A24. Provenance remains, but destiny changes.
An area cell born from two orthogonal lengths is no longer only "those two
lengths." It is now a lawful area basis that can measure any compatible area.

A25. Commutativity belongs to the folded count of the shared basis cell, not
necessarily to the pre-lift roles that generated it.

A26. If orthogonal consolidation preserves opposed endpoint roles instead of
stabilizing a shared interior cell, the result is passage-like rather than
interior-like.

A27. Directed segments are of this passage-like kind:

- they arise from two directed roles in one unified cell
- they carry start/end or inbound/outbound asymmetry
- they are not yet the same thing as a rotational or area-bearing space

A28. Higher dimensions therefore do not arrive by declaration.
They arrive when orthogonal tension becomes stable enough to support a new
basis cell.

## Part V. Laws, Units, And Encounters

A29. Units do not merely label values.
They constrain which laws are natural.

A30. Different unit families bias different lawful reads:

- accumulation
- expansion
- repetition
- partition
- occupancy
- transport
- calibration

A31. Unit bias is not the whole law.
The law is chosen at the encounter.

A32. A pin, site, or meeting point is where unit tendencies become enacted.

A33. The same structural arrangement may therefore yield different lawful
results under different enacted laws.

A34. Example:

- one axis as "stuff" and one as "repeat" naturally yields repeated stuff
- two axes as "expand" naturally yield growth of a shared cell

A35. So units bias what wants to happen, while laws decide what does happen.

## Part VI. Continuation And Time

A36. Because every extent is ordered, every lawful carrier is traversable.

A37. Time is the traversal of ordered structure.
Clock time is one special calibration of that more general fact.

A38. Interpolation is traversal through aligned value.

A39. Navigation is traversal through structure.

A40. Generation is traversal through unresolved relation under a law.

A41. Overflow, contradiction, or non-fit is continuation pressure.

A42. Continuation pressure discharges according to available structure:

- wrap if a repeat path is available
- reflect if the continuation reverses through available opposition
- branch if several continuations remain lawful
- merge if several paths rejoin one state
- lift if a new carrier is required
- tension if no honest settlement yet exists

## Part VII. Tension

A43. Tension is preserved unmet relation, not failure.

A44. Tension may be:

- value tension
- carrier tension
- law tension
- branch tension
- resolution tension

A45. Tension is what allows the system to remain truthful before settlement.

A46. Null, zero, and tension are different.

A47. Zero means a present zero-like read.
Null means absence.
Tension means present but unsettled structure.

A48. A lawful system prefers preserved tension over false collapse.

A49. Lift is one lawful resolution of tension, but not the only one.

## Part VIII. Minimal Consequence

A50. Mathematics can therefore be read as:

- ordered uncalibrated extents
- stabilized support cells
- calibrated values
- orthogonal tensions
- higher carriers born from stabilized shared basis cells
- laws enacted at encounters
- continuation through value, structure, and generation

A51. Value, structure, and generation are not arbitrary categories.
They are the three recurrent physical states of ordered relation:

- settled count
- visible arrangement
- lawful becoming

A52. Dimensions come into being because tension between real distinctions
eventually stabilizes a new basis cell that the old carrier could not hold.
